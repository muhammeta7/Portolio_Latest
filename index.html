ABOUT ME

I graduated with a degree in chemical engineering and like most, did not work in that field after school. After about a year of doing an unfulfilling job, I decided enough was enough. I took some time to travel and reflect on what I wanted to do for a career.

Upon returning from my trip, I decided I wanted to take on the world of web development and enroll in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. It has been a great experience to learn the field and it's something I find myself being passionate about. I have learned a ton of new technologies and coding resources and can't wait to implement that knowledge in my own work.
