ABOUT ME

I graduated with a degree in chemical engineering and like most, did not work in that field after school. After about a year of doing an unfulfilling job, I decided it was time for a change. I took some time to travel and reflect on what I wanted to do for a career. Upon returning from my trip, I decided I wanted to take on the world of web development.

I have been coding non-stop since and decided to enroll in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. It has been a great experience to learn the field and it's something I find myself being passionate about. I have learned a ton of new technologies and coding resources to implement in my own work. I can't wait to take on all of the new challenges the coding world has to offer me.
