ABOUT ME

I graduated with a degree in chemical engineering and like most, did not work in the field after school. After a year of working a mechanical engineering job, I knew it was time for a change. I took some time to travel and reflect on what I wanted to do for a career.

Upon returning from my trip, I decided I wanted to take on the world of web development and enrolled in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. My array of skills learned from the class includes front-end design to back-end databse manipulation to build full-stack applications. It has been one of the most rewarding changes I have ever made and I can't wait to implement my problem solving skills to this field.
